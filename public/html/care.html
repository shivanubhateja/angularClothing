All good things need love

The OC Tees are built to last. Treat it with care, and a single tee will look great for years. Here's how you do it -

Gentle, Cold Wash

Cotton shrinks quickly in hot water. Hand wash your March Tee in cold water or use the delicate cycle on your machine.

Dry in Shade

Sunlight dulls dyes that are used to color cotton. Line dry your March Tee in the shade, or tumble dry it on low.

Warm Iron

Cotton can easily get scorched under a hot iron. Always iron your March Tee on low to medium heat.

No Dry Cleaning

Chemicals are harsh on cotton fabrics. Bleaching and dry cleaning are bad for the planet and too intense for your March Tee. Just stick to the basics.
