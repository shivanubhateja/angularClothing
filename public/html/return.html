Returns

Returning an OC tee is as easy as ordering one. If you need an exchange or a refund, email help.orangeclips@gmail.com and tell us what's going on. We will help you with the process.

Prepare to return you OC tee

Make sure the tee is in good state. Unused, of course. The original bag and tags are intact.

Step 1

Put the packaged tee in the box. Don't have the original box? No problem. Find a good, strong box or envelope to put the OC Tee in.

Step 2

Print three copies of return slips from your order page*. Stick one firmly on the box. Hand over the remaining slips to the our delivery guy. You should be all set.

*Link in your order email.
