Returns

Returning an OC tee is as easy as ordering one. If you find any manufacturing defect in your tees, reach us at contact@orangeclips.com and tell us what's wrong with the tees. We will be happy to help you out.

Prepare to return your OC tee

If your tee is eligible for return, make sure the tee is in good state. Unused, of course. The original bag and tags are intact.

Step 1

Put the packaged tee in the box. Don't have the original box? No problem. Find a good, strong box or envelope to put the OC Tee in.

Step 2

Stick our address, communicated by our executive, firmly on the box. Hand over the box to the our delivery guy. You should be all set.
